Privacy Policy

Lucky Ones Meal Planner
Last updated: March 2026

This privacy policy describes how Lucky Ones Meal Planner ("the App") handles your information.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data you enter — including meal plans, pantry items, and shopping lists — is stored locally on your device only.

Camera Permission

The App requests access to your camera and photo library solely to allow you to add photos to your meal plans. These photos are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared with any third party.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements to non-premium users. Google AdMob may collect certain information to serve relevant ads. You can learn more about how Google uses data at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

In-App Purchases

The App offers a premium upgrade. Purchase transactions are handled securely by Google Play and we do not store any payment information.

Data Storage

All app data including meal plans, pantry items, shopping lists, and preferences are stored locally on your device using secure local storage. We have no access to this data.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
