Privacy Policy

ADB Tile
Last updated: May 2025

This privacy policy describes how ADB Tile ("the App") handles your information.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All information the App reads stays entirely on your device.

Local Device Access

To function, ADB Tile reads the following from your device only:

  • Wireless debugging state – whether Android wireless debugging is currently enabled, read from system settings.
  • Local IP address – your device's current Wi-Fi IP address, used to display the ADB connect string.
  • ADB port – the port Android assigns for wireless debugging, detected via system settings or local mDNS.

None of this information is sent off your device.

Permissions

  • ACCESS_WIFI_STATE – read your local Wi-Fi IP address.
  • INTERNET – required for mDNS service discovery on the local network to detect the ADB port. No data is sent to the internet.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – show a brief confirmation when the connect string is copied to the clipboard.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services.

Children's Privacy

ADB Tile is a developer tool intended for adults. It does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new effective date.
