NOTES LITE

Privacy Policy — Last updated: May 2026

Notes Lite is developed and published by Victain LTD. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

1. Data Stored on Your Device

Notes Lite stores all your notes, checklists, sketches, and settings locally on your device only. No note content is transmitted to our servers. This includes:

  • Text notes and checklist items
  • Freehand sketch drawings
  • Photos attached to notes
  • Voice memo recordings
  • Folders, tags, colours, and pin state
  • Reminder dates and times
  • App settings (theme preference)

All note data remains on your device and is never uploaded or shared without your explicit action (e.g. you choosing to share a note via the share sheet).

2. Camera and Microphone

Notes Lite requests access to your camera to attach photos to notes. Microphone access is used only when you record a voice memo. All media is saved locally on your device and is never transmitted to us.

3. Biometric Authentication

Notes Lite can optionally lock individual notes or folders using your device's biometric authentication (fingerprint or face recognition). Biometric data is handled entirely by your device's operating system — Victain LTD does not have access to it and it is never stored within the app or transmitted anywhere.

4. Notifications

With your permission, Notes Lite may send local notifications to remind you of note reminders you have set. These notifications are scheduled on-device and do not involve any external server.

5. Advertising

Notes Lite displays adverts served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's Advertising ID to show relevant ads. This is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalised ads at any time via your device settings under Privacy → Ads.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties, except as described in Section 5 (Google AdMob) or where required by law.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

All app data is stored locally on your device. You can delete individual notes within the app at any time. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data. Victain LTD does not hold copies of your notes on any server.

8. Children

Notes Lite is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will always be available at this URL with a revised date at the top.
