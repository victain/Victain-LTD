Privacy Policy

Victain Water Tracker  ·  Victain LTD  ·  Last updated: 30 March 2026

✅ No personal data collected by this developer
✅ No user data shared with third parties by this developer

Overview

Victain Water Tracker is built with your privacy in mind. The developer (Victain LTD) does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All data you enter in the app — water logs, goals, reminders, and streaks — stays entirely on your device.

Data stored on your device

The following data is saved locally and never leaves your device:

  • Water intake entries (amount and time)
  • Daily hydration goal
  • Weight (if entered for goal calculation)
  • Reminder schedule and preferences
  • Streak and achievement progress
  • Your name (if entered)

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app displays banner ads powered by Google AdMob. AdMob is an independent Google service that may collect your device's advertising identifier (Ad ID) to serve ads. This data is handled entirely by Google and is not accessible to Victain LTD.

You can opt out of personalised ads via your device settings:
Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID (Android 12+)
or Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation (older Android).

For more information see Google's Privacy Policy.

Permissions used

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — to send hydration reminders you configure
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM — for precise reminder timing on Android 12+
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS — optional, to prevent reminders being blocked by battery saving

No access to contacts, location, camera, microphone, or files.

Data deletion

You can delete all app data at any time from Settings → Reset all data within the app. Uninstalling the app removes all data from your device.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a revised date.
